Achievements

  • Placed 15th amongst the top 43 teams of the world in AUVSI Robosub 2014 competition. Emerged as the best team from India.
  • Winner of the National Student Autonomous Vehicle competition in 2014, organized by National Institute of Ocean Technology (NIOT).
  • The team won the Gold medal and silver medal respectively for the reports on Control systems of AUV and Mechanical Design of AUV in Student design competition conducted by NDRF.
  • The project was shortlisted amongst the top 44 projects across the nation and was presented at the National Fair of India Innovation Initiative (i3), jointly conducted by CII and DST.
  • The project was selected amongst the top 24 innovations across the country and was selected to appear on the show 'Power of Shunya - Challenge for zero', hosted by ET NOW TV News channel.
  • The team was placed 4th in Infosys Building the Enterprise Challenge, Chennai Region.
